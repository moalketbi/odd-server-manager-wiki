A bad mod, a corrupted save, an angry tribe — backups undo all of it.

<Step n={1} title="Open the Backups tab">
  Click **Backups** in the top navigation. You'll see a list of past snapshots, newest first.
  <Screenshot src="/odd-server-manager-wiki/screenshots/backups-tab.png" alt="Backups tab with snapshot list" />
</Step>

<Step n={2} title="Take a manual backup">
  Click **Backup Now**. The app pauses the world save, copies it, and resumes — usually under 10 seconds.
</Step>

<Step n={3} title="Restore from a snapshot">
  Pick a snapshot row → **Restore**. The current world is replaced. The server stops automatically, restores the snapshot, and restarts.
  <Screenshot src="/odd-server-manager-wiki/screenshots/restore-dialog.png" alt="Restore confirmation dialog" />
</Step>

<Tip>**Always take a manual backup before installing or removing a mod.** Mod changes are the #1 cause of save corruption.</Tip>
