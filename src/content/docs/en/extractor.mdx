After installing mods, the app's settings dropdowns won't yet show the new dinos, items, and engrams. The **Game Data Extractor** reads your mod files and tells the app what's there.

<Step n={1} title="Open the Extractor">
  Click the **archive icon** in the top-right header bar (tooltip: *Game Data Extractor*).
  <Screenshot src="/odd-server-manager-wiki/screenshots/header-extract-icon.png" alt="Game Data Extractor icon in the header" />
</Step>

<Step n={2} title="Scan and extract">
  The window shows a table of every mod installed across all your server profiles. Click **Extract All** at the top — the app reads each mod and writes the extracted data to its internal Game Data folder. Takes 30 seconds to a few minutes per mod.
  <Screenshot src="/odd-server-manager-wiki/screenshots/extractor-window.png" alt="Game Data Extractor window with mod list" />
</Step>

<Step n={3} title="Restart the app">
  After extraction, a banner appears at the top: *"Mods extracted. ASM needs to restart to load the new data."* Click **Restart now**.
  <Screenshot src="/odd-server-manager-wiki/screenshots/restart-banner.png" alt="Restart banner after extraction" />
</Step>

<Step n={4} title="Confirm the new content is available">
  Reopen any server tab and look at e.g. the spawn settings or harvest dropdowns — you should now see the modded dinos and items listed.
</Step>

<Tip>Re-run extraction whenever you **install a new mod** or **a mod updates**. Paths and content change between mod versions, and stale data leads to mysterious "where's that dino?" issues.</Tip>
